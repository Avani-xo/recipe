Follow the Steps precisely to prepare and cook the perfect Gnocchi

  1. Boil and drain the potatoes, then mash or pass through a ricer.
  2. Cut the snakes into pieces.
  3. Divide the dough into four even portions and shape into "snakes."
  4. Cut the snakes into pieces.
  5. Boil and drain the gnocchi.
  6. You can add the gnocchi into a soup or broth of your choice for a savoury and umami meal, or you can have it as it is.
